A Wookie you can depend on.

Chewbacca, known affectionately to his friends as Chewie, is a Wookiee warrior, smuggler, mechanic, pilot, resistance fighter, and is ready for new and exciting career opportunites!

Career Highlights

Chewbacca has a track record of success and over 250 years of industry experience. Having won numerious accomidations for his services in the Clone Wars, the Galactic Civil War, the Cold War, and the subsequent First Order-Resistance War, his military record is second only to the fabled warriors of Mandalor.

Outside of his tremendious record as a multi-war veteran, Chewbacca has shown excellence in the feilds of astro-navigation, starcraft piloting, and is considered a leader in the field of creative transportation. Chewbacca holds the Kessel Run record with his ship The Millenium Falcon at 11.5 Parsecs.
